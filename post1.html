Beach Camping in Brazil

Posted on October 20, 2025

Brazil’s coastline is full of hidden gems for beach camping enthusiasts. Whether you’re in Florianópolis, Bahia, or Rio de Janeiro, you’ll find secluded beaches where you can pitch your tent and enjoy a night under the stars.

The key to a great beach camping experience is preparation. Bring enough fresh water, sun protection, and lightweight gear. Avoid areas with high tides and check local regulations before setting up camp.

The best part? Waking up to the sound of waves, watching the sunrise over the ocean, and enjoying breakfast barefoot on the sand.
